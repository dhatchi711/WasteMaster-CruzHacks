Welcome to WasteMaster

This application created by four college sophomores, Ameya Mandale, Dhatchinamoorthi Kunde Govindarajan, Hari Siddhi Natraj Sankaranarayanan and Sri Siddhartha Madduri provides a solution to improper waste management amongst individuals. The application was created within a day at CruzHacks, during the pandemic of 2021.

The way to use the application is as simple as it sounds. Just simply upload a JPEG or PNG file of an item that you don't know where to dispose, and the WasteMaster will guide you to the right bin. It answers all your problems in improper waste management and helps you become more environmentally conscious!
